[Absolutiones]
O Lord Jesus Christ, graciously hear the prayers of Thy servants, and have mercy upon us, Who livest and reignest with the Father, and the Holy Ghost, ever world without end.
May His loving-kindness and mercy help us, Who liveth and reigneth with the Father, and the Holy Ghost, world without end.
May the Almighty and merciful Lord loose us from the bonds of our sins.

[Nocturn 1]
May the Eternal Father bless us with an eternal blessing.
May the Son, the Sole-begotten, mercifully bless and keep us.
May the grace of the  Holy Spirit enlighten all our hearts and minds.
In the unity of the Holy Ghost, may the Father and the Son bless us.

[Nocturn 2]
May God the Father Omnipotent, be to us merciful and clement.
May Christ to all His people give, for ever in His sight to live.
May the Spirit's fire Divine in our hearts enkindled shine.
Of all faults and sins may the power of the holy Trinity absolve us.

[Nocturn 3]
May His blessing be upon us who doth live and reign for ever.
God's most mighty strength alway be His people's staff and stay.
May He that is the Angels' King to that high realm His people bring.
He whose feast-day we are keeping, be our Advocate with God.
They whose feast-day we are keeping, be our Advocates with God.
She whose feast-day we are keeping, be our Advocate with God.
They whose feast-day we are keeping, be our Advocates with God.
She whose feast-day we are keeping, Mary, blessed Maid of Maidens, be our Advocate with God.
May he whose feast-day we are keeping, our glorious Father, be our advocate with God.

[Evangelica]
May the Gospel's holy lection Be our safety and protection.

[Evangelica9]
May the Gospel's glorious word Cleansing to our souls afford.

[Nocturn 3 12-25]
May the Gospel's saving Lord Bless the reading of His Word.
May the Gospel's glorious word, cleansing to our souls afford.
Christ That sent the Gospel preacher, In his meaning be our Teacher.
