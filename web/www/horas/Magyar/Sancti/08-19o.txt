Homily by St Austin, Bishop (of Hippo.)
!27th on the Words of the Lord.
The troubling about many things passeth away, but the love for the one thing remaineth. Therefore that which Mary hath chosen shall not But that which thou hast chosen, O Martha, it must needs follow, it must needs be understood, shall be taken away from thee. But it will be taken away from thee for thy good, that that which is better may be given unto thee. Work will be taken away from thee that thou mayest rest. Thou art on the voyage Mary is already in port.
