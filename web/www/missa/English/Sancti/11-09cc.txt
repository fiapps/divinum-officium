[Officium]
St. Theodore, Martyr

[Oratio]
O God, Who surround and protect us with the glorious profession of blessed Theodore, Your Martyr, grant us to profit by imitating him and to rejoice in his intercession.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
Accept, O Lord, the prayers and sacrificial gifts of Your faithful people, that, through these rites of love and devotion we may pass over to the glory of heaven.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
Grant, we beseech You, O Lord, by the intercession of blessed Theodore, Your Martyr, that what we taste with our lips we may consume with a pure heart.
$Per Dominum
