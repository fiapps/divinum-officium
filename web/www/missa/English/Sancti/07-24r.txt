vide C6;
Gloria

[Secreta]
Graciously receive, O Lord, the sacrifices offered to You, by the merits of Blessed Christina, Your Virgin and Martyr, and grant that they may become for us an abiding source of strength.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
Filled with the abundance of Your divine gift, we beseech You, O Lord our God, that, by the intercession of Blessed Christina, Your Virgin and Martyr, we may live forever by the participation thereof.
$Per Dominum
