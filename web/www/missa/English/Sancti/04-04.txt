[Oratio]
O God, Who granted to Your people blessed Isidore as a minister of salvation, grant, we beseech You, that we who cherished him on earth as a teacher of life may be found worthy to have him as an intercessor in heaven.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
May the pious prayer of holy Isidore, thy Bishop and illustrious Doctor, be not wanting to us,~
O Lord, but make our offerings acceptable to thee and ever win for us thy mercy.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
May blessed Isidore thy Bishop and illustrious Doctor intercede for us, O Lord, that this thy~
sacrifice may obtain for us salvation.
