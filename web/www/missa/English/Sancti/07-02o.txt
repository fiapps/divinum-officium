[Commemoratio Oratio]
! SS. Processus and Martinian
O God, Who surround and protect us with the glorious~
profession of faith of Your Martyrs Processus and Martinian, grant us to profit~
by imitating them and to rejoice in their intercession.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Secreta]
! SS. Processus and Martinian
Accept our prayers and gifts, O Lord, and, that they may be made worthy in Your~
sight, may Your saints help us by their prayers.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Postcommunio]
! SS. Processus and Martinian
Filled with the offering of Your sacred Body and Your precious Blood, we beseech~
You, O Lord, our God, that what we have done in holy devotion may become an~
assurance of our redemption.
$Per eundem
